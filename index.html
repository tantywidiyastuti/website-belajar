About Us

At Beautree, we embody the essence of “Natural – Functional – Thoughtful Design.” Our brand is dedicated to creating beauty cases that not only serve the practical needs of Makeup Artists (MUAs) but also align with a sustainable and thoughtful approach to craftsmanship.

Makeup Artists often face the challenge of managing tools in cramped spaces, requiring ergonomics, portability, and efficiency. That's why we've chosen pine wood and bamboo as our primary materials – renewable, biodegradable alternatives that reduce environmental impact while offering durability and a natural aesthetic.

Our Values

  • Environmentally Friendly: Using sustainable materials to minimize our carbon footprint.
  • Ergonomic & Modular Design: Adjustable compartments for personalized organization.
  • Collaboration with Local SMEs: Empowering local artisans and supporting community-driven production.
  • Supporting SDGs: Contributing to Decent Work and Economic Growth (SDG 8), Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure (SDG 9), and Responsible Consumption and Production (SDG 12).

We believe in empowering MUAs from beginners to professionals, fostering an urban lifestyle that values sustainability and thoughtful design.
